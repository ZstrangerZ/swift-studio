Frequently Asked Questions

Can I try Swift Studio before purchasing?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial on all plans with no credit card required. You'll have access to all features with limited usage during the trial period.

What happens if I exceed my plan limits?

We'll notify you when you reach 80% of your limits. If you exceed them, you can purchase additional capacity or upgrade to a higher plan. We never cut off your service without warning.

Do you offer educational or non-profit discounts?

Yes, we offer special pricing for educational institutions, non-profits, and startups. Contact our sales team for more information.

How secure is my data on Swift Studio?

We take security seriously. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We're SOC 2 and GDPR compliant, and we never use your data to train our own models.
